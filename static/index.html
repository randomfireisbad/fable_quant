    Day Trader Desk (Paper)

    An autonomous agent practicing on the paper portfolio: each session it reviews positions, scans its universe with the statistical models, trades within risk limits (max 25% per position, 10% cash floor, vol-scaled sizing), and journals what it learned. When enabled, sessions run on a schedule during US market hours. Quotes are ~15min delayed, so it trades intraday-swing setups, not scalps. Paper money only.

    Model Improvement Loop

    Every trade records the model's signals at entry. Closed round trips are attributed by rating, score, and source below — the evidence base for improving the models. Ask Claude (in Cowork or via the connector) to review this; changes only happen when the sample size justifies them, and every change is recorded in the improvement log with its evidence.
